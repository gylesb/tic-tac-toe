Welcome to Daniel and Gyles' game of Tic-Tac-Toe

In case you don't know how to play, here are some rules:

  • Choose to play against either another player or the computer.
  • Player One places an "X" on one of the 9 squares by clicking on the corresponding square
  • Player Two places an "O" on one of the 9 squares by clicking on the corresponding square
  • You cannot hit the same square more than once per game
  • First player to get three in a row wins!
